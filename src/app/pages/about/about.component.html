Disclaimer

We are a non-profit website and community dedicated to modding custom music into video games.

We, in no way, shape or form, advocate for video game or media piracy. You should buy your video games to support the music creators. The content hosted on our website is meant for non-commercial personal-use, injection into and to supply modding projects that are aimed and structured towards being used with legally bought games!

This website is a continuation of smashcustommusic.com, which was shut down in August 2019 after operating for almost 10 years. We are not associated with the original team.

The original website was created to house all custom music files created to modify the Super Smash Bros. Brawl game for the Wii, which was a very popular scene circa 2009.

We are still adding new features and content, if you'd like to contribute, please read the Help page or visit our Github repository below.
