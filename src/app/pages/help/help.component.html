How to contribute What you need to know to contribute with our project.

To create a new account with the sole purpose of creating and uploading new BRSTM music to our archive, please visit our old website smashcustommusic.net to create your account and start contributing.

Read through the account registration terms and log to that website to upload musics there, as we still have not implemented user logins and uploads to this website.

If you want to contribute with tools and/or with the website, please contact us directly in our Telegram group.

I have claims of authorship and want some credit. What should I do?

If you do have inquiries regarding existing content, such as editing requests, deletion proposals or claims of authorship/credit etc, please mail us at:

management{{'@'}}smashcustommusic.net

Please provide us with some form of verification of authorship or representation when reaching out to us about your work. We take your requests seriously and will respond as fast as possible, but we may dismiss messages that lack authenticity.
